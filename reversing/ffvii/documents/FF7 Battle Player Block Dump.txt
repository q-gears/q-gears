--------------
00 00 00 00	Normal
00 00 01 00	Barrier
00 00 02 00	MBarrier
00 00 04 00	Reflect
00 00 80 00	Berserk
00 01 00 00	Haste
00 02 00 00	Slow
00 05 00 00	Stop
00 08 00 00	Frog
00 10 00 00	Mini
08 00 00 00	Poison
01 00 00 00	Dead
10 00 00 00	Sadness
20 00 00 00	Fury
80 00 00 00	Silence

OR	Order
-------------
08	Front
48	Back
C8	Back against Enemy
Add 20	Defend

Example	:- If in front row and in defend, the value should be 28

CH	Character No.
LV	Player Level
PA	Physical Attack
MA	Magic Attack
DX	Dexterity
LK	Luck
PD	Physical Defense
MD	Magic Defense
CMP	Current MP
MPM	MP Max
CHP	Current HP
HPM	HP Max

STI	Status Immune To
------------------------
Same values as ST
